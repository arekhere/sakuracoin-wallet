Have your Monacoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Monacoin Wallet is the first mobile Monacoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Monacoin amount in MONA and mMONA.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Monacoin via NFC, QR-codes or Monacoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Monacoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Monacoin balance.

You can find more about Monacoin at
http://monacoin.org

If you want to contribute to Monacoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/keystore00/monacoin-wallet-new

This is a fork of the popular Bitcoin Wallet by Andreas Schildbach and was converted to Monacoin by me.
Find the original at
