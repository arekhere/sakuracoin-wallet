Have your Sakuracoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Sakuracoin Wallet is the first mobile Sakuracoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Sakuracoin amount in SKR and mSKR.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Sakuracoin via NFC, QR-codes or Sakuracoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Sakuracoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Sakuracoin balance.

You can find more about Sakuracoin at
http://sakuracoin.org

If you want to contribute to Sakuracoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/ohac/sakuracoin-wallet

This is a fork of the popular Bitcoin Wallet by Andreas Schildbach and was converted to Sakuracoin by me.
Find the original at
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.schildbach.wallet
